About Waha Mobility

Empowering clean, efficient, and smart electric mobility.

Who We Are

We are an emerging electric mobility company focused on creating robust, modular EV platforms that meet the everyday needs communities and critical defense requirements.

Mission

Our mission is to engineer simple, durable, and adaptable EV platforms that deliver reliable performance in challenging terrains and essential field environments

What We Do

We design and manufacture simple, durable EV platforms engineered to perform in real-world field conditions. Our modular chassis architecture enables seamless integration of interchangeable tools and mission-specific add-ons, supporting efficient mobility in Tier-2/3 regions and reliable deployment in tactical environments.
